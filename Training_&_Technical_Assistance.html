Training and Technical Assistance

 

The Bay Area Community Land Trust (BACLT) offers training, education and technical assistance to new co-ops, existing co-ops and to groups that want to convert to a Limited Equity Housing Cooperative (LEHC) or other resident controlled housing model.   BACLT also provides presentations for public education.



Training and technical assistance is one of the most important factors for a successful cooperative.  New co-ops must establish a solid foundation and prepare new members to take on the challenges of operating residential housing.  Many co-op members have never owned a home and have not had the opportunity to learn how to manage property.  Existing co-ops often need to refresh and retool to keep enthusiasm for cooperative living alive.


BACLT can help a collective house convert to a co-op.
