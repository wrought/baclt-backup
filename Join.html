Join/Donate to BACLT

 

The Bay Area Community Land Trust is a membership organization.  If you support our mission of permanently affordable cooperative housing, please become a member.  Annual dues are a modest $25 a year (although we are happy to accept donations of larger amounts; feel free). 


You can send us a check by U.S. mail to Bay Area Community Land Trust, P. O. Box 1004, Berkeley, CA 94701. 


Or you can join by e-mailing us at membership@bayareaclt.net and sending us dues by Paypal (using the Join button).

You can pay your dues or make a donation of any amount with the Donate button and  fill out this handy dandy form to give us your info  Sorry, you can’t do them both on the same form (funky, we know, but we can’t afford to be slick.)
